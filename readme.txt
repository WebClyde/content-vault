Protect and archive your WordPress content automatically. Track history, detect broken links and keep timeline of your pages.

== Description ==

Your website content is valuable. But what happens if a page changes, a link breaks, or you need proof that your content existed at a specific time?

Content Vault is a simple WordPress plugin that helps you protect, archive, and track your content history automatically.

It creates a secure record of your posts and pages whenever they are published or updated, so you always have a verifiable archive and timeline of your content.

Whether you are a blogger, business owner, or digital agency, Content Vault helps you keep your content safe and documented.

### Why Use Content Vault?
Websites constantly change. Pages are updated, links disappear, and sometimes you need to prove when something was published.

Content Vault solves this problem by automatically saving archive records of your content and monitoring important changes.

### With Content Vault, you can:
- Keep a historical record of your content
- Track when updates were made
- Protect your website from broken external links
- Maintain a reliable archive of your pages

All of this happens quietly in the background without interrupting your workflow.

### Who Is Content Vault For?
Content Vault is perfect for bloggers, publishers, digital agencies, content creators, businesses managing important web pages, and any website that relies on external references.


== Features: ==

- **Automatic Content Archiving**
 Automatically archives posts and pages whenever they are published or updated.

- **Content History Timeline**
 View a timeline of archive records for each page to see how your content evolved.

- **Timestamped Archive Records**
 Each archive includes a verified date and time stamp.

- **Broken Link Monitoring**
 Detect external links that stop working and help users access archived versions.

- **Archive Status Tracking**
 Monitor whether archive requests are successful, pending, or failed.

- **Simple Dashboard**
 A clean dashboard to monitor archives, failures, and link checks.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/content-vault` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Access the feed at `https://your-site.com/wp-json/merchant-ai/v1/feed`.

== Usage ==

The feed is available at the `https://your-site.com/wp-json/merchant-ai/v1/feed` endpoint.
It will prompt a file of `products.json` which can be downloaded.

You can submit this URL or the downloaded file to OpenAI's Merchant interface.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What does Content Vault do? =
Content Vault automatically archives your WordPress posts and pages whenever they are published or updated. It keeps a record of your content history and helps ensure that your important content is preserved over time.

= Why should I archive my content? =
Web content can change or disappear over time. Archiving helps you maintain a historical record of your pages and provides proof of when your content existed. This can be useful for documentation, transparency, and long-term content protection.

= Does Content Vault slow down my website? =
No. Content Vault is designed to work quietly in the background. The plugin runs archive tasks efficiently without affecting the normal performance of your website.

= Does the plugin work automatically? =
Yes. Once the plugin is installed and activated, Content Vault automatically archives your posts and pages when they are published or updated. You don't need to manually archive content.

= Can I see the archive history of a page? =
Yes. Content Vault provides a simple timeline where you can view archive records of your posts and pages, including timestamps.

= What happens if an external link on my site breaks? =
Content Vault monitors external links and helps detect when a link stops working. When possible, it can guide users to archived versions of those pages.

= Is Content Vault suitable for large websites? =
Yes. Content Vault is designed to work with small blogs, business websites, and large content-driven websites.

= Is Content Vault free? =
The core version of Content Vault is free and provides essential archiving and monitoring features. Additional advanced features may be available in future updates.
